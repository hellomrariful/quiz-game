Which Indian state is known as the "Land of Spices"?

The Spice Trade Legacy

Kerala has historically been known as the "Land of Spices" due to its rich heritage in spice cultivation and trade. The state's unique geographical features and climate make it ideal for growing various spices like cardamom, pepper, cinnamon, and cloves.
